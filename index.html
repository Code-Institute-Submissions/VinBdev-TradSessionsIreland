Where/When were we founded?

This group was founded in 2002 after a the Fleadh Ceoil (Most famous traditional Irish music festival) took place in Listowel county Kerry. The music group started quiter naturally by playing music in the Horseshoe bar and restaurant in the midst of a great Flead Ceoil week.
