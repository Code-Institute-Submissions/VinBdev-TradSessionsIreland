    Where/When were we founded?

    This group was founded in 2002 after a the Fleadh Ceoil (Most famous traditional Irish music festival) took place in Listowel county Kerry. The music group started quite naturally by playing music in the Horseshoe bar and restaurant in the midst of a great Flead Ceoil week.

  • Read the story of Irish Music

    Why were we founded?

    We founded this trad music group in order to encourage anyone from anywhere in Ireland to learn and play Irish music. Whilst Irish music is still very popular in Ireland, the main function of this group is to give young and old musicians an opportunity to play with other musicians and overall encourage the celebration of Irish music and culture.

    Who can play with us?

    ANYONE! This trad group is encouraging adn welcoming of anyone who is interested in attending our events. Even if you have no abilities to play an instrument, come along have some criac and even join in a sing along!

    What music do we play?

    Most commonly we play and celebrate traditional Irish music. Howver this is a multi-cultured group of talented musicians and welcoming people. Therefore we also branch out often and encourage people to do ballads or songs of different cultures as we belwive all cultures should be celebrated and appreciated by all.
